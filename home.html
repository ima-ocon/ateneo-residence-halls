LIVING AT THE ATENEO RESIDENCE HALLS

Welcome to the Residence Halls! Conveniently located at the heart of the Loyola Heights Campus, the University’s Residence Halls is the preferred student residence providing Ateneans with a sense of community as it celebrates cultural diversity. Through its well-managed environment, programs, and culture. It provides integrative formation that promotes wholeness and wellness.

There are four (4) residences within the Residence Halls Complex. The Cervini Hall (for males) and Eliazo Hall (for females) are exclusive for First Year students and the North Tower (for males) and South Tower (for females) of the University Dormitory are for upperclassmen, exchange students, and graduate students.

VISION AND MISSION

The Ateneo Residence Halls envisions a dynamic community of resident students who live by the ideals of the Ateneo de Manila University as a Filipino, Catholic and Jesuit institution.

Guided by the values espoused by the First Companions (RH Patron Saints): Ignatius of Loyola offering his sword to patient governance (leadership), Francis Xavier’s zealous mission (service), and Peter Faber devoting his life to compassionate spiritual direction (spirituality), the Ateneo Residence Halls seeks to

  • Provide a safe and secure living environment conducive to college life;
  • Offer formation programs that will develop the person of the residents in the academic, socio-cultural, and spiritual aspects;
  • Form life enriching relationships;
  • Promote wellness, physical development, skills and talent development;
  • Advocate service opportunities and social involvement.
